## Recording Tips
- Use QuickTime → File → New Audio Recording, or your phone's Voice Memo app
- One recording per scene — speak at a natural pace, don't rush
- Quiet room, close to the mic
- When all files are ready, tell Claude and the audio will be synced to the video
