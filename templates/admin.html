Backup Database

Secondary Backup Location

If set, every backup is also copied to this path. Use an absolute path accessible inside the container (e.g. a mounted volume or NAS).

Upload CSV

Upload a new Microsoft Forms CSV export. Only Mist architecture rows are imported — non-Mist rows are skipped. Duplicate entries (by ID) are ignored.

Upload Engagement CSV

Upload an engagement tracking CSV export. Records are matched to existing customers by Customer Name. Duplicate customer names in the CSV are merged — conflicting field values are concatenated. Rows with no matching customer are skipped and reported.

Expected columns: Customer Name, Sales Engineer, State, Catalog, BU PLM Sponsor, BU PLM TME, Current Status, Next Actions, Get Well Plan Link
